# Navigation Rationale

The design brief called for clear top-level navigation: Garden, Essays, Notes, Patterns, Library, About, and Now.

In this initial Quartz 4 implementation, those destinations are exposed in three stable places:

1. Homepage curated navigation block.
2. Footer global links available site-wide.
3. Garden and section index pages as curated hubs.

Compromise: Quartz's stock layout does not provide a native top horizontal global nav without custom component work. For this initial version, I favored simplicity and maintainability over custom component complexity.
