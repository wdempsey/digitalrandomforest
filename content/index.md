Digital Random Forest is where I think in public about how AI is changing academic data science — across the student, researcher, practitioner, and manager lenses I move between.

The working argument under most of these pages is one claim: the next durable advantage in data science won't come from access to a better model. It will come from the ecosystem around the model — the workflows, evaluation loops, reproducible traces, and human judgment that compound over time. Model access isn't the moat. The learning ecosystem is.

A good place to start is the [[garden/essays/why-data-sciences-future-is-an-ecosystem-story|ecosystem essay]] below. The notes are where the same ideas are still forming.
