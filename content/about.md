I build AI workflows for data scientists and spend most of my time translating between code, methods, and messy research questions.

This garden is where I keep that translation visible.

It is public, incremental, and intentionally unfinished.

What you will find here:

- essays that try to make a clear argument
- notes that capture working hypotheses
- patterns for reusable research workflows
- library entries that summarize academic papers with practical intent

What you will not find here:

- product documentation
- polished tutorials for every tool
- a private vault exported to the web
